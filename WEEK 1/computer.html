COMPUTER INTRODUCTION:

A computer is an electronic device that processes and stores data to perform a variety of tasks. It consists of hardware components, such as a central processing unit (CPU), memory, storage devices, input/output devices, and a motherboard, which connects and facilitates communication between these components.

BASIC OPERATION:

Computers can perform complex calculations, manipulate data, store and retrieve information, and execute programs or software. They are capable of running various operating systems, such as Windows, macOS, or Linux, which provide a user interface to interact with the computer.

MORE:

Computers have become an integral part of modern life, used in a wide range of fields including business, education, entertainment, communication, scientific research, and many others. They can be found in desktop form, laptops, tablets, smartphones, and even embedded within other devices such as cars, appliances, and wearable technology.
