Egg Sandwich


Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 2 eggs
- Butter or oil for cooking
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: cheese, ham, bacon, lettuce, tomato, or other desired toppings

Instructions:
1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add a little butter or oil.
2. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
3. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and cook until scrambled, stirring occasionally.
4. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
5. Spread butter or any desired condiments on the bread.
6. Place the scrambled eggs on one slice of bread and add any additional toppings.
7. Top with the other slice of bread.
8. Cut the sandwich in half if desired and serve.
