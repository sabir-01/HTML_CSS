Arabian salad


Arabian salad, also known as Arabic salad or Fattoush, is a refreshing and colorful salad popular in Middle Eastern cuisine. It is typically made with a combination of fresh vegetables, herbs, and toasted or fried bread.

Here's a basic recipe for Arabian salad:

Ingredients:
- 2 cucumbers, diced
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 cup chopped fresh mint
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 pita bread, toasted or fried until crispy
- Sumac (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:
1. In a large salad bowl, combine the cucumbers, tomatoes, bell peppers, red onion, parsley, and mint.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
3. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss well to coat.
4. Break the toasted or fried pita bread into bite-sized pieces and add them to the salad.
5. Toss again to distribute the bread throughout the salad.
6. Sprinkle sumac on top for added flavor and garnish.
7. Serve the Arabian salad immediately as a refreshing side dish or light meal.

Note: You can customize the salad by adding other ingredients such as radishes, lettuce, or olives. Some variations also include feta cheese or grilled chicken for extra protein. Feel free to adjust the quantities and ingredients to suit your taste preferences.

Enjoy your Arabian salad!
