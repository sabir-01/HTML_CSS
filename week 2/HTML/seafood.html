Seafood Sandwich


Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 150g cooked seafood (such as shrimp, crab, or lobster), chopped
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: lettuce, tomato, or other desired toppings

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, combine the cooked seafood, mayonnaise, lemon juice, celery, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
2. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
3. Spread butter or any desired condiments on the bread.
4. Place the seafood mixture on one slice of bread and add any additional toppings.
5. Top with the other slice of bread.
6. Cut the sandwich in half if desired and serve.
