Pasta is a versatile and beloved dish enjoyed in various cuisines around the world. It can be made with a wide range of ingredients and sauces. Here's a basic recipe for a classic pasta dish:

Classic Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce:

Ingredients:
- 8 ounces (225g) spaghetti or pasta of your choice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (14 ounces or 400g) crushed tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional toppings: grated Parmesan cheese, fresh basil leaves

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente (cooked but still firm to the bite). Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant but not browned.
4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir in the dried basil and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld together.
6. Add the cooked pasta to the tomato sauce and toss until the pasta is evenly coated.
7. Remove from heat and serve the pasta in bowls.
8. If desired, top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves for extra flavor and garnish.

Note: This recipe serves as a basic foundation, and you can customize it by adding cooked meat, vegetables, or other ingredients to the sauce. Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta shapes and variations of sauces to create your own favorite pasta dishes.

Enjoy your classic spaghetti with tomato sauce!
