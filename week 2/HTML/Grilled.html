Grilled Cheese


Ingredients:

2 slices of bread
Butter for spreading
Cheese slices (such as cheddar, Swiss, or American)
Instructions:

Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat.
Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
Place one slice of bread, butter side down, on the skillet.
Add cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
Place the other slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese.
Cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and the cheese starts to melt.
Flip the sandwich with a spatula and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese is fully melted.
Remove from the skillet, cut the sandwich if desired, and serve.
Feel free to customize these recipes by adding or substituting ingredients to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy your sandwiches!
