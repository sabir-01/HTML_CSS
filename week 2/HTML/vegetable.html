vegetable salad

Here's a recipe for a refreshing and nutritious vegetable salad:
Ingredients:
- 2 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.), washed and torn into bite-sized pieces
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1 bell pepper (any color), thinly sliced
- 1 carrot, grated
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
- 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
- Handful of fresh herbs (such as parsley, basil, or mint), chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

For the dressing:
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar (such as balsamic or apple cider)
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard (optional)
- 1 clove of garlic, minced (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cucumber, bell pepper, carrot, cherry tomatoes, red onion, black olives (if using), and feta cheese (if using). Toss gently to combine.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice or vinegar, Dijon mustard (if using), minced garlic (if using), salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat the vegetables evenly.
4. Sprinkle the fresh herbs over the salad and give it a final toss.
5. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
6. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
7. Serve the vegetable salad as a side dish or as a light and refreshing main course.
8. You can customize the salad by adding other vegetables of your choice, such as radishes, celery, or avocado.

Enjoy your fresh and delicious vegetable salad!
