Chicken bolognese


Chickpea bolognese is a delicious and hearty vegetarian or vegan alternative to the classic meat-based Bolognese sauce. It replaces the ground meat with chickpeas for a protein-rich and flavorful dish. Here's a recipe for chickpea bolognese:

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 can (14 ounces or 400g) crushed tomatoes
- 1 can (14 ounces or 400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 cup vegetable broth or water
- 8 ounces (225g) pasta of your choice
- Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:
1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
2. Add the diced onion, carrots, and celery to the skillet and sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
3. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir in the dried basil, dried oregano, dried thyme, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper.
5. Add the drained and rinsed chickpeas to the skillet and stir to combine.
6. Pour in the vegetable broth or water to loosen the sauce and bring it to a simmer.
7. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken.
8. While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
9. Once the sauce has thickened, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
10. Serve the chickpea bolognese sauce over the cooked pasta.
11. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.
12. Enjoy your flavorful and satisfying chickpea bolognese! Feel free to customize the recipe by adding other vegetables, such as bell peppers or mushrooms, to the sauce.
